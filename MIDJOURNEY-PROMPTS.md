# SEHFSC — Midjourney Image Prompts

**Purpose:** Generate images for sehfsc.org — Zone 0 page, homepage, and prevention pages

**Rules for all prompts:**
- Southern California suburban neighborhood aesthetic — stucco homes, tile roofs, San Elijo Hills style
- Natural afternoon light, clear sky, slight haze typical of San Marcos CA
- Documentary/editorial photography style — NOT commercial real estate photography
- No people in frame
- Aspect ratio 4:3 to match site photo slots unless noted otherwise
- Use --style raw and --s 50 for grounded, photographic results
- Bill said the board will take real local photos eventually. These are high-quality placeholders.

**Technique for matching pairs:** Generate the BEFORE image first. Then use it as an image prompt with the AFTER description to keep the same composition.

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Generate BEFORE first.** Get an image you like, then use it as a reference for the AFTER prompt.
2. **Use --style raw** to avoid the overly polished Midjourney look. We want documentary realism.
3. **Use --s 50** (low stylization) to keep it grounded and photographic.
4. **If a result looks too perfect,** add "slightly worn" or "lived-in" to the prompt.
5. **Match the light.** Both prompts specify the same time of day and angle.
6. **San Elijo Hills specifics:** Beige/tan stucco, terracotta or gray tile roofs, hillside lots, native chaparral in background, clear San Diego sky.
7. **Bill's board will take real photos.** These Midjourney images are placeholders until the board photographs actual Zone 0 work in the neighborhood.
